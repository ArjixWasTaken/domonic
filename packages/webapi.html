URL¶

url is a wrapper around the urlparse and urlencode functions in python.

from domonic.webabi.url import URL

myurl = URL("http://www.google.com/search?q=domonic")
print(myurl.host)
print(myurl.query)
print(myurl.query.q)
print(myurl.query.q.value)
